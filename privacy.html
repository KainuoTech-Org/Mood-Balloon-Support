Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

English Version

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mood Balloon ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name (optional), and authentication credentials when you create an account
  • Emotional Records: Emotion type, intensity, trigger descriptions, and timestamps
  • Assessment Results: Test responses, scores, and completion dates from psychological assessments
  • Meditation Activity: Session duration and completion status

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT access your camera or photo library
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT collect contact lists
  • We do NOT record audio without permission

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Display your emotional journey and assessment history
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Diagnose technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Cloud-based storage for cross-device access
  • Local device storage for offline functionality
  • Industry-standard encryption for all data transmission
  • Strict access control mechanisms

We implement regular security audits and do not store payment information.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share limited data only:

  • With cloud service providers for data storage and app functionality (under strict data protection agreements)
  • When required by law or legal process

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data in the app
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Control: Manage data collection in settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@kainuotech.com

7. Children's Privacy

Mood Balloon is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymous Data: Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be retained for improvement purposes

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notification or email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Email: contact@kainuotech.com

  • 我們不會訪問您的相機或相冊

  • 我們不會收集聯絡人列表
  • 我們不會未經許可錄音

我們使用您的資訊來：

  • 提供和維護應用程式功能
  • 顯示您的情緒旅程和測評歷史
  • 改善應用性能和用戶體驗
  • 診斷技術問題

4. 資料儲存與安全

您的資料使用以下方式安全儲存：

  • 雲端儲存以支援跨設備訪問
  • 本地設備儲存以支援離線功能
  • 所有資料傳輸使用業界標準加密

5. 資料分享

我們不會向第三方出售、交易或出租您的個人資訊。

我們僅在以下情況共享有限資料：

  • 與雲端服務提供商共享(用於資料儲存和應用功能,已簽署嚴格的資料保護協議)
  • 法律或法律程序要求時

6. 您的權利

您有權：

  • 訪問：在應用中查看您的所有資料
  • 匯出：請求您的資料副本
  • 刪除：移除您的帳號和所有相關資料
  • 控制：在設定中管理資料收集

如需行使這些權利,請聯絡：contact@kainuotech.com

7. 兒童隱私

心願球適用於13歲及以上的用戶。我們不會故意收集13歲以下兒童的資訊。

8. 資料保留

  • 活躍帳號：帳號活躍期間資料會被保留
  • 已刪除帳號：所有資料將在30天內永久刪除
  • 匿名資料：匯總的匿名分析資料可能會被保留用於改進目的

9. 隱私政策變更

我們可能會不時更新本隱私政策。重大變更將通過應用內通知或電子郵件傳達。

10. 聯絡我們

如對本隱私政策有疑問,請聯絡:

電子郵件：contact@kainuotech.com
