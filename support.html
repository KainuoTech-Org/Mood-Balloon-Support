Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create an account?

A: You can sign in with Apple ID for the fastest setup. Alternatively, you can use guest mode to try the app with local-only data storage.

Q: Is my data private and secure?

A: Yes! All your data is encrypted and stored securely. Local data stays on your device, and cloud data (if you create an account) is protected with strict access controls. We never sell your personal information.

Q: Can I delete my account and data?

A: Absolutely. Go to Settings â†’ Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data within 30 days. You can also export your data before deletion.

Q: Does the app work offline?

A: Most features work offline, including emotion tracking, viewing history, and assessments. The AI chat feature requires an internet connection.

Q: How do I change the language?

A: Go to Settings â†’ Language. Mood Balloon supports English, Traditional Chinese, and Simplified Chinese.

Q: Is this app a replacement for therapy?

A: No. Mood Balloon is designed for self-awareness and emotional wellness support. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact local emergency services or a mental health professional.

Troubleshooting

Problem: App crashes or freezes

Solution: Try restarting the app. If the problem persists, restart your device. Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed.

Problem: Can't sign in with Apple ID

Solution: Ensure you're signed in to iCloud on your device. Check your internet connection. Try signing out of iCloud and signing back in.

Problem: Data not syncing

Solution: Check your internet connection. Make sure you're signed in to your account. Try logging out and logging back in.
