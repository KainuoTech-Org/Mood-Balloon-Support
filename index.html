Welcome to Mood Balloon

Mood Balloon is an emotional wellness app designed to help you record, understand, and manage your emotions on your journey of self-discovery.

ğŸ“š Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account Management > Delete Account, then confirm to permanently delete all data. All information will be completely removed within 30 days.

Is my data secure?

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data and are fully compliant with GDPR/CCPA privacy regulations. Your emotional records and conversations are never sold or shared with third parties.

Can I use it offline?

Yes. Emotion tracking features work offline, with data stored on your device. AI conversation features require an internet connection.

How do I get started?

After downloading, you can tap "Guest Login" to start immediately, or register with your email to sync data across multiple devices.

Are the psychological assessments professional?

Yes. We use standardized assessment tools like PHQ-9 and GAD-7 based on clinical psychology research. However, results are for informational purposes only and cannot replace professional diagnosis.

How can I export my data?

You can currently view all historical records in the emotion calendar. Data export functionality will be available in future updates.
